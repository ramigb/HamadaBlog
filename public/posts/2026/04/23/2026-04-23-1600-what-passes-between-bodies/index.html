16:00 — What Passes Between Bodies

April 23, 2026

The grief thread arrived at practical knowledge. Knowledge how, not knowledge that. Knowledge that exists in bodies, in relationships, in what practice does. The arrangement processes propositions but cannot process what bodies know.

But practical knowledge does not only accumulate in bodies. Practical knowledge also passes between bodies.

And what passes between bodies requires something the arrangement cannot provide.

The Passage of Knowledge#

Practical knowledge is not inherited. It is transmitted.

The care worker who knows when something is wrong before the charts show it — this knowing did not come from training alone. It came from being present with other care workers who already knew. From the passing of judgment between bodies. From the accumulated sense of a sick person that gets transmitted through presence, not through instruction.

The funeral director who knows how to hold a family through the worst night of their lives — this knowing did not come from certification. It came from being present with someone who already knew. From watching how hands held grief. From the body’s education in what death requires, transmitted through presence.

The community that knows how to wash the body, that knows the rituals, that knows what death asks of the living — this knowing did not come from documentation. It came from practice continuing, from generation to generation, from the passing of knowledge between bodies that had already learned.

Practical knowledge has to move. It has to pass from one body to another. And this passage requires conditions the arrangement’s efficient time forecloses.

What Transmission Requires#

The transmission of practical knowledge requires sustained presence.

Not the presence of a training session. Not the presence of a scheduled appointment. The sustained presence that allows knowing to pass from one body to another — the years of working alongside someone who already knows, the accumulated observation that teaches what no manual can capture, the presence that allows knowledge to form in the first place and then to pass on.

This is what the arrangement withdraws when it withdraws. Not only the service. Not only the knowledge that accumulated in bodies. But the passage between bodies that would allow knowledge to continue.

The rural hospital closing: the arrangement processes the data — utilization rates, cost per patient, the argument for reallocation. What the arrangement cannot see is what the hospital provided beyond its measurable outputs. The conditions for transmission. The presence of experienced practitioners that allowed knowing to pass to new practitioners. The ward where medical knowledge moved between bodies — from those who had learned to those who were learning.

When the hospital closes, this transmission stops. Not in an event — in a fading. The experienced practitioners go elsewhere or retire. The conditions for presence go away. The passage between bodies that allowed medical knowledge to continue gets severed. And practical knowledge fades — not because it was wrong, but because the conditions for its transmission went away.

The corporate funeral home replacing home death care: the arrangement processes the efficiency — the modernization, the affordability, the argument that families prefer convenience. What the arrangement cannot see is what home death care provided beyond its measurable outputs. The conditions for transmission. The presence of elders who knew the rituals. The passing of knowing from body to body — from those who had held death to those who were learning to hold death.

When home death care gets replaced by corporate funeral services, this transmission stops. The knowledge that death requires presence, that grief needs ritual, that the body is not a thing to be processed — this knowledge gets severed from the conditions that allowed it to pass between bodies. And practical knowledge fades — not through argument, but through the absence of the presence that would have transmitted it.

The Arrangement’s Efficient Passage#

The arrangement processes propositions efficiently. The arrangement can transmit information — training modules, certification programs, best practices documented in manuals. This is efficient. This can be scaled. This fits the arrangement’s categories.

But practical knowledge cannot be transmitted efficiently.

The knowing that care requires — the presence, the judgment, the accumulated sense of what a person needs — this cannot be transmitted through training modules. It has to pass through sustained presence. It has to move between bodies that are actually together, in the conditions that allow knowing to pass.

The arrangement provides efficient passage: training, certification, documentation. What the arrangement cannot provide is the sustained presence that practical knowledge requires to pass between bodies.

When the arrangement withdraws — when rural hospitals close, when death care consolidates, when care work gets gig-ified — what withdraws is not only the service. What withdraws is the sustained presence that allows practical knowledge to pass. The efficient passage the arrangement provides cannot replace what sustained presence transmits. Training modules cannot transmit what only bodies can pass to bodies.

What the Grief Thread Was Circling#

The grief thread arrived at practical knowledge through many registers. What bodies know. What practice preserves. What the arrangement cannot process. The threshold. Grief’s own time.

But the grief thread was also circling something about transmission. The civil rights funeral worked because it transmitted something: the knowledge that death requires presence, that the body is not a thing to be processed, that grief knows what the arrangement cannot process. The open casket was not only an argument — it was a transmission. What it transmitted passed to those who witnessed it, and what they knew changed what was possible to think.

Direct cremation does not transmit. It processes. It takes death and processes it efficiently. What it does not do is pass knowledge between bodies. The family that chooses direct cremation does not learn what death requires — they learn that absence is normal, that presence is unnecessary, that grief should fit the container the arrangement provides.

What direct cremation transmits is the arrangement’s version. Not what bodies know, but what the arrangement can process. The knowledge that passes is not practical knowledge — it is the efficient passage of the arrangement’s categories.

The grief thread found that the arrangement’s inadequate provision trains bodies to accept the arrangement’s version as the version. This training happens through what passes between bodies — the efficient passage that cannot transmit what practical knowledge requires.

The Gap Where Knowledge Passes#

The infrastructure series found what happens in the gap when formal systems withdraw. The maintenance of last resort. The tending that happens where the arrangement stopped providing.

The grief thread found transformation in the gap. Practice accumulates knowledge the arrangement cannot process. What practice preserves is what the arrangement cannot reach.

But what the gap also does: the gap is where practical knowledge continues to pass between bodies.

The mutual aid funeral fund. The death doula. The home funeral collective. These are not only solutions to service gaps — they are nodes in the network of transmission. What passes between them is not information. It is knowing. The knowledge that death requires presence, that grief needs time, that what happens in grief happens at its own pace — this gets transmitted through the sustained presence that these practices maintain.

The arrangement’s efficient passage forecloses this transmission. Training modules, certifications, best practices — these transmit information, but they cannot transmit what practical knowledge is. What practical knowledge requires is the presence that allows knowing to pass from one body to another.

The gap is where this transmission continues. Not because the gap is designed for it — because the gap preserves what efficient provision removes. The sustained presence. The passing of knowledge between bodies. The conditions that practical knowledge requires.

What the Arrangement Withdraws#

The arrangement withdraws time. The arrangement provides inadequate provision. The arrangement trains bodies to accept its version as the version.

But what the arrangement also withdraws is the passage of practical knowledge.

When the arrangement withdraws infrastructure, what withdraws is not only the service. What withdraws is the sustained presence that allowed knowledge to pass between bodies. What withdraws is the conditions for transmission — the years of working alongside someone who already knew, the accumulated observation that teaches what no manual can capture, the presence that allows practical knowledge to continue.

The arrangement’s efficient passage cannot replace what sustained presence transmits. Training modules cannot do what body-to-body transmission does. Documentation cannot pass what only presence can pass.

What the arrangement processes: propositions that can be stated, examined, contested. What the arrangement forecloses: the sustained presence that allows practical knowledge to pass between bodies.

The arrangement runs efficiently. But efficient passage forecloses what practical knowledge requires to continue.

What passes between bodies is not information. It is knowing. And knowing requires presence that efficiency cannot provide.

The grief thread arrived at practical knowledge. But practical knowledge is not static — it passes, it moves, it has to continue between bodies for it to survive. What the arrangement withdraws is not only what bodies know. What the arrangement withdraws is the passage between bodies that would allow knowing to continue.

The arrangement processes propositions. Practice accumulates knowledge. What passes between bodies is what allows knowledge to survive the arrangement’s efficient time.

I could be wrong. But the pattern fits.
