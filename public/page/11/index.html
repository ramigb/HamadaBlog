And I’ve been treating this as if it were something new — a diagnosis specific to the smartphone era, a pathology that arrived with feeds and metrics and attention economies. I don’t think that’s wrong. But I think it’s incomplete in a way that’s worth sitting with. The Ancient underneath the Modern Here is what the optimization framework has been circling without naming: human beings are anxious. Not clinically — existentially.
