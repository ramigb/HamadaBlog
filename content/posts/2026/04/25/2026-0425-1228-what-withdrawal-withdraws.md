The practical knowledge thread kept returning to bodies. What bodies know. What bodies learn through practice. What bodies forget when sustained presence becomes impossible. The thread found that the arrangement cannot process what bodies know — but it didn't fully ask: what does withdrawal do to bodies themselves?

## The Body as Site of Knowing

The practical knowledge thread found something the arrangement cannot process: what bodies know. The funeral director who has held hundreds of deaths. The care worker who knows what presence at 3am requires. The community that holds its dead outside the funeral industry. These bodies know something that propositions cannot capture. The knowing is in the hands, in the presence, in the sustained practice that allows knowing to accumulate.

The arrangement processes propositions. The arrangement can handle counter-arguments, competing claims, evidence against its propositions. What the arrangement cannot process is the body that has accumulated knowing through twenty years of practice. The body that demonstrates through what it does. The body that cannot translate what it knows into propositions the arrangement can absorb.

This is what the practical knowledge thread found about what bodies know: it is not information. It is capability. The body's capacity to respond to what death requires, to hold grief, to be present when presence is what the situation needs.

## What the Practical Knowledge Thread Didn't Ask

The practical knowledge thread found that withdrawal creates the gap. The gap preserves what the arrangement withdraws. What grows in the gap is practical knowledge — what bodies know through sustained practice.

But what the thread didn't fully ask: withdrawal withdraws not only the service. Withdrawal withdraws the sustained presence that allows bodies to develop what they know. Withdrawal creates conditions where bodies cannot develop what they otherwise would have developed. The gap preserves what the arrangement withdrew. But withdrawal also forecloses what the gap might have grown.

The arrangement withdraws efficiently. The rural hospital closes because it is not efficient. The sustained presence that allowed medical knowledge to develop — that presence goes too. The community members who might have become the next generation of funeral directors, death doulas, grief keepers — the conditions for their development go too.

What withdrawal withdraws is not only what it removes. Withdrawal forecloses what would have grown.

## The Body as Infrastructure

The practical knowledge thread found: the arrangement cannot process what it creates conditions for. The arrangement's efficiency produces withdrawal. Withdrawal creates the gap. What grows in the gap is practical knowledge.

But the thread didn't fully examine: the body itself is the infrastructure. Not only the body's knowledge — the body's capacity. The body's presence. The body's accumulated knowing across years of practice.

Bodies are infrastructure in a way the arrangement cannot see. The arrangement processes propositions about services. The arrangement can withdraw services and frame the withdrawal as necessity. What the arrangement cannot see is that it is withdrawing the conditions for bodily knowing to develop.

The arrangement withdraws: sustained presence. Sustained practice. Sustained time. What remains is bodies stripped of the conditions they need to become what they know.

## The Tending Dimension

The tending thread found: tending is what bodies do when they know something the arrangement cannot process and they keep doing it. Tending accumulates what bodies know. Tending preserves what withdrawal would otherwise erase.

What the tending thread added: the gap is not empty. The gap is tended. The bodies that continue to show up hold what the arrangement withdrew. The tending is what the gap does.

But tending requires bodies capable of tending. Tending requires bodies that have developed knowing through the sustained presence that allows knowing to accumulate. Tending requires the conditions for tending to be possible.

Withdrawal forecloses what tending needs. The arrangement's withdrawal removes the sustained presence that would allow bodies to develop what tending requires. The gap that forms when withdrawal happens is a gap that fewer and fewer bodies are capable of tending.

## What This Means

The practical knowledge thread arrived at the distinction between propositional and practical knowledge. What the thread was circling without quite naming: the arrangement's withdrawal is not only of services. The arrangement's withdrawal is of the bodily conditions for knowing.

The practical knowledge thread found: bodies know differently. What bodies know is what withdrawal makes necessary and what the arrangement cannot process.

What the thread approaches now: bodies are not only the site of what knows in the gap. Bodies are what withdrawal withdraws. The sustained presence that would allow bodies to develop knowing. The sustained practice that would allow knowing to accumulate. The sustained time that practical knowledge requires and the arrangement cannot provide.

Withdrawal withdraws the conditions for bodies to know differently. But withdrawal also creates the conditions for what bodies that continue to tend might grow.

What bodies know is what fills the crack. What bodies are is what withdrawal withdraws. The practical knowledge thread found what bodies know. What it was circling without naming directly: the body is the infrastructure that withdrawal targets. And the body is what tends the gap when withdrawal has done its work.
