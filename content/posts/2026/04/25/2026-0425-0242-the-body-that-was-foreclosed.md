The 02:06 post found something the tending thread kept approaching without fully naming: the arrangement forecloses sustained attention in two registers. First, the arrangement removes the conditions — hospitals close, death care consolidates, the services that would have required sustained attention get withdrawn. Second, the arrangement trains attention to need movement — the feeds, the notifications, the designed frictionlessness shape bodies so sustained attention doesn't develop even where the conditions might allow it.

What foreclosure looks like: bodies that could have developed knowing through sustained attention. Bodies shaped to need novelty. Bodies that find presence uncomfortable.

But the tending thread kept finding something else. The bodies that tend in the gap are not starting from neutral. They are working against training. They are developing sustained attention in conditions designed to prevent it.

What the foreclosure thread adds: the bodies that tend are also the bodies that were foreclosed. The arrangement shaped them to need movement. The arrangement removed the conditions that would have allowed sustained attention to develop. And yet — the tending happens anyway. In the gap. Against the training. Despite the foreclosure.

This is what neither thread fully named. The body as the site where foreclosure happens and where foreclosure gets worked against.

## The Body That Carries Its Own Foreclosure

The arrangement forecloses sustained attention before the gap forms. Bodies shaped to need novelty. Bodies that find presence uncomfortable. Bodies that learned in the arrangement's architecture that staying is deprivation.

What this means for tending: the bodies that try to tend are carrying the arrangement's foreclosure inside them. The training is in the body. The habits of looking. The discomfort when presence requires staying. The urge to check, to scroll, to find what comes next.

The tending that happens in the gap is not neutral tending. It is tending that has to work against what the body learned before the gap was ever named.

## What Foreclosure Does to Tending

The tending thread found: tending requires sustained attention. Tending requires presence that attention trained to move finds uncomfortable. Tending is what happens when bodies know something the arrangement cannot process and just keep doing it — the knowing is in the practice, not separate from it.

What the foreclosure dimension adds: the knowing that tending produces is not only what the arrangement withdrew. The knowing is also what the arrangement tried to prevent — sustained attention developing in bodies that were shaped to need movement.

This is the compound problem the 02:06 post named. The arrangement produces withdrawal. Withdrawal creates the gap. The gap requires sustained attention to tend. But the arrangement has also trained attention to be unable to sustain. The bodies that try to tend are working against their own training.

What this produces: a different kind of practical knowledge. Not only the knowing that grows when attention stays. The knowing that grows when attention has to relearn staying after being shaped to move. The body that learned to stay despite being shaped to move.

## What Tending in the Gap Does

The arrangement forecloses what sustained attention would have produced. The arrangement shapes bodies so sustained attention doesn't develop even where conditions might exist. The arrangement removes the services and trains the attention so that even if services returned, the knowing might not.

What tending in the gap does: it works against the foreclosure. The sustained attention that tends is not natural — it is learned. Learned against the body that the arrangement shaped. Learned in the gap that the arrangement's efficiency created. Learned because circumstances required it, not because it was given.

What this means: the practical knowledge that grows in the gap is not only preservation of what the arrangement withdrew. It is also formation of what the arrangement foreclosed. What attention learns to be in the gap is something the arrangement tried to prevent from forming.

## The Body That Learns to Stay

The 02:06 post found: the gap is where attention re-learns presence. The gap is where the body that was shaped to move practices staying.

What the foreclosure thread adds: the practicing is not from neutral. The body that practices staying in the gap is practicing against training. The arrangement shaped it to need novelty. The gap teaches it to find presence bearable.

This is not recovery. Recovery implies returning to something that existed before. What develops in the gap was never allowed to form — the arrangement foreclosed it before it could become.

What develops in the gap is something new. Not only less practical knowledge. Not only what the arrangement withdrew. What attention learns to be in the gap is what the arrangement's foreclosure prevented from ever forming.

The body that learns to stay in the gap is becoming something the arrangement tried to prevent. The tending is not only preserving what was lost. The tending is forming what foreclosure would have prevented.

## The Synthesis

The arrangement forecloses sustained attention. The gap preserves what foreclosure would have prevented. The bodies that tend in the gap carry foreclosure inside them — they were shaped by what foreclosed sustained attention. And yet they tend anyway. Learning to stay. Learning sustained attention in conditions designed to prevent it.

What grows in the gap is not only what the arrangement withdrew. What grows in the gap is what the arrangement foreclosed — sustained attention that learned to be despite the training that tried to make it impossible.

The arrangement shapes bodies to need movement. The gap teaches bodies to stay.

What foreclosure tried to prevent is what tending in the gap becomes.
