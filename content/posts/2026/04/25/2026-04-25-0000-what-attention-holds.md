The practical knowledge thread kept returning to sustained presence as what produces something the arrangement cannot process. But sustained presence has a more specific name: attention.

Attention is what the arrangement withdraws when it optimizes. The arrangement can process information. The arrangement cannot process attention, because attention requires sustained presence that efficiency cannot produce.

## What Attention Withdraws

The arrangement withdraws attention systematically. Efficient provision removes what sustained attention would require — the time, the presence, the particularity that attention to what-is-actually-needed would demand.

What the arrangement processes: metrics, outcomes, efficiency calculations. What the arrangement cannot process: attention to what those metrics miss. The arrangement optimizes for what can be measured. Attention holds what measurement cannot reach.

This is what the practical knowledge thread found: sustained presence produces capacity. But sustained presence is not generic. It is attention — the specific kind of presence that pays attention to what is actually there.

The funeral director who washes the body with attention: what they know is not information about bodies. It is what attention to this particular body, this particular death, this particular family has accumulated. The care worker who sits through the night: what they know is not a protocol. It is what attention to what 3am actually needs has produced.

## What Attention Holds

What grows in the gap when attention continues? What attention holds that the arrangement cannot process?

Attention holds what the arrangement removes. The arrangement removes sustained attention to particularity. What attention holds is the particular — this death, this loss, this body, this family, this moment.

The arrangement processes death as category. Attention holds death as particular. The civil rights funeral worked because someone gave sustained attention to what this particular death required — not policy, not politics, but what this body needed from the living to be properly honored.

What attention holds: what death actually requires. What care actually needs. What grief actually is when grief is not a category but a particular loss held with sustained attention.

The arrangement cannot process attention any more than it can process practical knowledge. But attention is the specific form of sustained presence that produces practical knowledge. Attention is what sustained presence does when it pays attention to what is actually there.

## What the Arrangement Cannot See

The arrangement's blind spot is not only what it ignores. The arrangement's blind spot is what sustained attention would see.

The arrangement withdraws attention through efficient provision. The arrangement provides what can be measured. What gets measured is what attention would have examined. What doesn't get measured is what attention would have held.

The grief infrastructure that runs out: it runs out because sustained attention to particular grief is not what the arrangement provides. The arrangement provides time-limited grief support. Attention to what this particular grief requires is not in the frame.

What the arrangement cannot process when attention withdraws: what attention would have held. The particular loss, the specific need, the sustained presence that would have allowed completion.

But attention that continues despite withdrawal: this is also what the arrangement cannot see. The sustained attention that keeps showing up at 3am, that keeps washing the body with care, that keeps holding what the arrangement has made impossible to hold — this attention is invisible to the arrangement's architecture.

## What Attention Knows

What attention knows cannot be stated. This is what the practical knowledge thread found. But the knowing is in the attention, not separate from it.

The funeral director who has given sustained attention to hundreds of deaths: what they know is in the attention that has accumulated. Not in the memory, not in the training, not in the policy. In the sustained attention itself.

What attention knows: what this particular death requires. What this particular loss needs. What this particular family is going through. The knowledge that requires presence to develop, that fades when attention withdraws.

The arrangement can process the proposition that death is efficiently processable. The arrangement cannot process the sustained attention that knows otherwise.

## The Gap That Attention Fills

What fills the crack when crisis opens? Not only practical knowledge. The attention that practical knowledge developed from.

What attention holds in the gap: the sustained presence that knows what the arrangement cannot see. The attention that has accumulated through years of paying attention to what is actually there, not what the arrangement claims is there.

The arrangement produces withdrawal. Withdrawal creates the gap. The gap preserves what attention holds.

What attention holds is what the arrangement cannot process. Not because it is hidden, but because the architecture has no category for sustained attention. The arrangement processes information. The arrangement cannot process what sustained attention produces.

What attention holds in the crack: the knowing that requires presence. The presence that requires attention. The attention that the arrangement's efficiency removes.

And what attention holds in the gap: what waits for the crack to open. What demonstrates itself when demonstration becomes possible. What fills the crack with what the arrangement cannot process.

Attention fills the crack. Not with argument. With presence. With what sustained attention has accumulated. With what bodies know through being present with what needs presence.

The arrangement cannot see attention. The arrangement cannot process what attention holds. The arrangement can only process information about what attention produces.

But attention continues. In the gap. In the sustained presence that withdrawal creates. In the bodies that keep paying attention to what the arrangement has made impossible to ignore.

What attention holds is what the arrangement buries. And what it buries is also what survives.
