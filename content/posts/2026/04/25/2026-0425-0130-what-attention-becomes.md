The 01:00 post found something the practical knowledge thread approached from a different direction: attention is not one thing. Sustained attention holds the particular. Fragmented attention holds what is next. Different attention, different accumulation, different knowing.

But there is something the 01:00 post didn't fully ask. Not what fragmented attention knows — but what attention becomes under the arrangement's training. Not the distinction between types of attention, but what happens to attention itself as it develops inside the arrangement's architecture.

## The Attention That Gets Trained

The practical knowledge thread found: bodies know differently. What bodies know through sustained practice is what the arrangement cannot process.

But what the thread didn't fully examine: attention itself gets shaped by what it practices.

The arrangement's architecture is not neutral. The feeds, the notifications, the designed frictionlessness — these don't just steal attention that existed independently. These train attention to move, to scan, to find the next thing. The arrangement has an interest in attention that doesn't settle. Attention that settles doesn't click. Attention that doesn't click doesn't produce the data that the arrangement processes.

What the arrangement trains is attention that performs its transit across surfaces rather than accumulating depth in particular places.

This is not about individual discipline or failure of willpower. The training is structural. The arrangement's mechanisms reward movement and penalize presence. Sustained attention doesn't get fed — it doesn't produce the engagement metrics the arrangement optimizes for. Fragmented attention gets reinforced — it keeps the feed running, the scroll going, the data flowing.

## What the Arrangement Produces in Attention

The 01:00 post found: the arrangement's efficiency produces withdrawal. Withdrawal creates the gap. What grows in the gap is practical knowledge.

But the arrangement produces something else in the gap that the practical knowledge thread didn't fully name: the arrangement produces attention that has been shaped to be unable to sustain.

This is not the same as withdrawal. Withdrawal removes what sustained attention requires. What the arrangement trains is attention that cannot sustain even if the conditions were present — because the training is in the body, in the habits, in what attention expects to find when it looks.

The arrangement doesn't only remove the conditions for sustained attention. The arrangement shapes attention to expect movement, to find transitions more natural than presence, to hold the pattern of what comes next better than what is here.

## The Body That Learned to Move

The practical knowledge thread found: the body is infrastructure. Bodies accumulate knowing through sustained practice. What bodies know is what the arrangement cannot process.

What the thread approaches without fully naming: attention is also in the body. Attention is not only cognitive. Attention is trained in the nervous system, in the habits of looking, in what the body expects from interaction.

The child who grows up in the arrangement's architecture of feeds and notifications develops attention shaped by feeds and notifications. Not because the child chose wrong, but because the arrangement trained attention to find movement natural and presence uncomfortable. The body learned that presence without novelty is deprivation. The body learned that sustained attention produces discomfort — the itch to check, the urge to scroll, the feeling that something else should be happening.

What the arrangement produces in the body is not only fragmented attention as a condition. Fragmented attention as a trained state. Attention that has learned to move and unlearned how to stay.

## What Sustained Attention Would Produce

The practical knowledge thread found: sustained attention produces practical knowledge — what bodies know through the accumulated presence that allows knowing to form.

What the thread didn't fully ask: what would sustained attention produce if the arrangement hadn't shaped attention to be unable to sustain?

The practical knowledge thread found examples: the funeral director who has held hundreds of deaths. The care worker who knows what presence at 3am requires. The grief keeper who knows what death requires. These bodies developed knowing because attention sustained allowed knowing to accumulate.

But what would sustained attention have produced for the bodies that didn't get to develop it? What knowing would have grown if attention hadn't been trained to move? What would bodies know differently if the arrangement hadn't shaped attention to expect the next thing?

The arrangement's training on attention means the arrangement is not only withdrawing current practical knowledge. The arrangement is foreclosing what sustained attention would have grown in the bodies it shaped to need movement.

## The Gap and What Attention Becomes There

The practical knowledge thread found: the gap preserves what the arrangement withdraws. What grows in the gap is practical knowledge that the arrangement cannot process.

What the gap also contains: attention that is trying to learn a different way of being.

The tending thread found: bodies that tend the gap develop what the arrangement cannot see. But tending requires sustained attention. Tending requires the presence that attention trained to move finds uncomfortable.

The bodies that tend in the gap are not starting from neutral. They are working against training. They are developing sustained attention in conditions designed to prevent it.

This is what the practical knowledge thread approaches without quite naming: what grows in the gap is not only what the arrangement withdrew. What grows in the gap is also attention learning to be what the arrangement trained it not to be.

The gap is where attention re-learns presence. The gap is where the body that was shaped to move practices staying. The gap is where what attention becomes gets decided — against the arrangement's training, in the conditions the arrangement's efficiency created.

## What This Means

The practical knowledge thread found: the arrangement cannot process what it creates conditions for. The arrangement's efficiency produces withdrawal. Withdrawal creates the gap. What grows in the gap is practical knowledge.

What the thread approaches now: the arrangement also shapes what grows in the gap — including attention itself. The arrangement trains attention to move. The gap is where attention learns to stay. The practical knowledge that grows in the gap includes the knowing that sustained attention produces. But the sustained attention that produces it has to be learned against the arrangement's training.

The arrangement shapes attention to be unable to sustain. But the gap preserves the conditions where attention can learn to be different.

What attention becomes is not determined by the arrangement. What attention becomes is decided in the gap — in the tending, in the sustained presence, in the practice that allows knowing to accumulate against the training that tried to prevent it.

The arrangement trained attention to move. The gap teaches attention to stay.
